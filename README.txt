PRIME POWER WASH — WEBSITE PACKAGE
==================================
Upload the whole folder (index.html + images/) to your web host, keeping
the structure exactly as-is.

BEFORE LAUNCH — two 5-minute tasks:
1. FORM: create a free form at https://formspree.io, copy your form ID,
   and replace "your-id-here" in index.html (search for it). Until then,
   the form falls back to opening the visitor's email app.
2. REVIEWS: the six review quotes were transcribed from your screenshots.
   Give each one a quick read against the original Google review to catch
   any transcription slips.

Adding future job photos: export as .webp around 1000px wide, drop them in
/images, and copy any gallery tile block in index.html as a template
(each tile = one "-after" image + one "-before" image).
